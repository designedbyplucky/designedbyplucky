Enquiry

The design consultation is a very important part of the design process. We like to start with a free 30 minute discovery call or in studio discovery session where our prospective client has the opportunity to express the vision they have for their design, ask questions, and learn a bit more about what to expect before booking the respective design.

    How Much Do You Charge?

    It is a genuine concern of every client to know their financial commitment before embarking on a project. I wish there were a simple answer to this; however, design services are not so easily quantifiable, as requirement varies for each project but we have all the starting prices in our socials.Follow us Today!

    How long before I see my first designs

    Depending on the type of project or design requirements, you should see initial design concepts anywhere from 48 hours to a few weeks after the start of the design process

    How will I see My Designs?

    I can send you basic design concepts via email as attached linked files (JPG,PNG or PDF).

    Can you recreate my old printed design?

    Yes. We can recreate any design from a photo or printed page.

    Do you keep my designs on File?

    For six months following your project, I keep your design in an active archive should you lose or damage your files or require follow-up work. After this period, I archive your files, and any retrieval at this point may include a nominal cost.

    What is the client's involvement in the design process?

    We encourage the client to bring in any samples, color combinations/swatches, and ideas they may have to help us get an idea of style before starting to work with new clients. During the design process, the client is asked to approve certain colors and fonts before work continues. Once the design project is ready for print we will submit a final proof for the customer’s approval before submitting to production. Once a job is in production, the job cannot be cancelled. At the conclusion of a project, the client may request the artwork in various formats if payment was made for design services.

    What is the payment schedule, and how do I pay?

    For the design process to commence the client should pay 60% of the agreed amount upfront.The rest should be paid after both our consultants and our clients have fully agreed about the final design.This is after the client has been shown a preview of the final design and all the revisions have been made.
